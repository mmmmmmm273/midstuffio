tutorial:

Maximum Opposition Bet Value (MOBV) is the most an opposition can bet
against you during a gamble.

MOBV is rerolled every time you get a fish. There is a minimum that will
gradualy increase the MOBV as you buy more ranks.

Buying a rank will deplete all of your cash even if it exceeds the rank requirement.

You cannot sell if you are Rank C and above.

You get access to more monetization as your rank increases. (see below)

Double Cash is a simple tech that lasts 30 seconds. A (2x) will appear next
to your cash while it's activated

Fishing Inflation is a hard to grasp tech. When activated, MOBV will always
reroll as the minimum according to your current rank.

It's very recommended to use Fishing Inflation when your inventory is almost full.

Coin Flip completley changes your chances, for better or for worse. While activated,
fishing now has a 60% chance to be common, 40% chance to be legendary, and
10% chance to be mythical.

Random fish, whose rarities are Epic and above, will appear in the auction. They can
have as much as an added 400$ to their real value, or be listed as low as 1$.

To reroll the auction, you must either buy the listed fish or use the two provided buttons.
Skewering the auction will always reroll into a mythical fish.

Bribing the auctioneer functions similarly to gambling selected fish, using the same
MOBV. The only difference is that, instead of comparing with selected fish, it compares
with the amount you type in.

MOBV Formula (x is a random integer): min < x < 1500 + (min / 2)

cash when losing an auction bribe. The only way to increase the auction's value is
to bribe any number that doesn't make sense.
